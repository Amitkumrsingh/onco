About

OnCo is an online application that helps students who are preparing for different exams including IIT JEE (mains and advanced), board exams, different state level competitive exams, exams in which PCM is given.

The services of this app are totally free of cost. You can ask doubts, take free test series, counseling, and some more features. If you don't want to avail these features, you can refer our app to someone who needs them as all the services are FREE OF COST!

We want to provide free and qualitive education for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our Aim

We aim to provide free education to all students. We are aware that there are plenty of applications and websites currently working and doing well in the field of education. We will have to compete with them. But, you can say that they are providing their materials at some cost and claim it as the prime content of their apps.?

For preparation, you will require some test series and quizzes but the cost of availing of these services is so high that many of us can't afford them. So, we aim to provide free test series and quizzes to the aspirants. Generally, you have to pay for asking your doubts and questions; we are also planning to provide facilities to ask doubts and questions without any paid subscription along with online counseling. We shall also run online classes in the next phase of our plan.

Why Free ?

We all have faced dfficulties at the time of preparation. We don't want anyone else to be faced those difficulties.
Education in India is so costly that even many of middle class families can't afford it. So here we are for the social cause, for the students and for the nation.

Everything Free

YES ! Everything we provide is free of cost. You don't need to pay any money for any of the service.

Is This Only For IIT JEE

We are not only for IIT JEE. Our Experts always try their best to help students. Currently we aim to provide help related to PHYSICS | CHEMISTRY | MATHEMATICS. So any service related to these three will given by us.
